Rorschach Test Platform - Privacy Policy

What information do we collect?

Rorschach test platform collects information about you from Facebook via the graph API.
After the acceptance of Facebook application your name, surname and Facebook ID are stored in our DB to identify you on further visits.

The other information collected, is about your network of contacts and their mutual connections. These information are downloaded from Facebook after an explicit request of the user who asks to compute his/her sociological indexes in the profile section of our application.

What do we use this information for?

Rorschach test platform uses the information collected for different purposes, all internal to Rorschach test platform itself.
Your information, whether public or private, will not be sold, exchanged, transferred, or given to any other company for any reason whatsoever, without your consent.

The information collected are used:

  • to personalize your experience;
  • to gather sociological information that could be helpful in evaluating the psychological profile;
  • to show you statistics, graphs and social data that could raise interest and boost social interactions.

How do we protect your information?

Data is stored in secure datastores on Google App Engine. Access to data is reserved to the user owner of the info and, with a higher granularity, to psychologists who administer and create tests on the platform.

Do we use cookies?

We use cookies only to manage session to speed up performances of the platform. The Rorschach test platform, however, can be visited also with cookies disabled, loosing performance optimisation.
The cookie does not store any relevant user information but is used only for session management.

Third party links?

Rorschach test platform does not include third party links or website pages.
